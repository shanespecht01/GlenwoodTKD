Youth 6:30pm - 7:15pm

Come in clothes you feel comfortable to move in. Expect to work on learning what a Taekwondo class consists of, Taekwondo techniques (kicks, blocks, punches, throws, falling), Forms (patterns to help you master the techniques), Workouts, Stretching, Sparring, and Self-Defense.

Events:

Because we are part of the Schutz Martial Arts Association and are friends with many schools and associations in the Midwest, we have been invited to participate in martial arts tournaments all over the region. Also through our connections, we often have the opportunity to take part in various types of Martial Arts training from experts from around the world. These opportunities are announced in class and will be posted on Social Media.
